Smoked Ribs

Tender Smoked Ribs

These ribs are simple to make and are extremky filling. With very little effort you will produce ribs that will be falling off the bone.

Ingredients List
  • Bone in pork ribs
  • Table spoon vinegar per rack of ribs
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
Let's Get Cooking
  1. Remove the membrane from the bone side of the ribs.
  2. coat the ribs with vinegar and let sit in th refridgerator for four hours.
  3. Bring ribs back to room temperature and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Smoke ribs at 225 degrees farenheit for three hours or until internal temperature reaches 175 degrees farenheit.
  5. Once cooked let ribs rest for thirty minutes. Then serve and enjoy.
