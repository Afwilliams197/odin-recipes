Baked Chicken

Effortless Oven Baked Chicken

This chicken recipe is for the person that doesnt have much time but still wants a healthy meal. This chicken only requires a few ingredients and the bare minimum whn it comes to supervision. All you need is a timer loud enough to tell you when its done.

Ingredients List

  • Uncooked chicken pieces
  • olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Time to make dinner

  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees farenheit
  2. lightly coat chicken with olive oil and the salt and pepper to taste
  3. Place chicken in the oven for 45 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  4. Let cool and enjoy
