Chili

A hearty chili for a grownup lifestyle

This chili is exactly as you remembered growing up. It will help keep you warm on cold days. Pack it in a thermos to help you get throuhj the harshest winter days.

The following recipe uses the simplest and most affordable ingredients. If you are a busy body it can be prepared quickly and reheated easily. Be sure to share this recipe if you like it.

Ingredients List
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 16 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup celery chopped
  • Preferred Spices to taste
Lets Get Started Cooking
  1. Heat pan on stove to medium/high setting and add olive oil.
  2. Combine garlic, yellow onion and celery until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and stir until cooked throughly then set aside
  4. In a pot add tomato sauce and seasoning.
  5. Add meat and vegtables and cook on mdium for thirty minutes stirring occasionally.
  6. Add whatever toppings you like and enjoy.
